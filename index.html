FOREX FACADE

As global trade increases, the foreign exchange market has become increasingly important to facilitate cross-border commerce.This graph is meant to show the relationship between exchange rates and macroeconomic factors. By selecting different "Currency" pairs and "Macro Data" indicators, you will be able to render different graphs to understand the interaction between the two. Most notably, the US Dollar/Japanese Yen is commonly used to forcast global economic conditions. You can see more detailed value by hovering over the chart rendering.
